Welcome to JP School

Jana Priya Boarding School is an English medium school established in 2056 B.S . It is located at a very convenient place at Kalagau, Surkhet. The school has stimulating, vibrant and genial environment. It is free from harmful dust and sound pollution. There is enough space/ground for extra co-curricular activities. This institution is established with sole iron determination for imparting quality education to the students. Jana Priya prides in being one of the top schools in Kathmandu. We provide Life-wide Quality Education with excellence to create a new generation of leaders and professionals with a caring and responsible attitude to society, inside and outside the typical spectrum.
